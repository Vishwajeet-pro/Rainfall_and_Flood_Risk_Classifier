Understanding Rainfall Impact

Light Rainfall (0-50mm)

Normal monsoon or local showers. Infrastructure remains stable. Suitable for agricultural activities.

Moderate Rainfall (50-150mm)

Heavy monsoon or sustained rain. Increases river water levels. Drainage systems become stressed. Caution advised.

Heavy Rainfall (150-300mm)

Extreme weather event. Rivers overflow. Siltation accelerates. Coastal areas face storm surge. Emergency protocols activate.
